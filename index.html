Indiagram builds PrivateClaw — a self-hosted AI gateway that keeps your keys, prompts, and data on a server you own.

What PrivateClaw does

PrivateClaw provisions a small cloud server (Hetzner, DigitalOcean, or Vultr) that acts as a private gateway for your AI tools. Your API keys and traffic stay on hardware you control instead of a third-party SaaS.

  • Bring-your-own cloud provider — no vendor lock-in
  • API keys live on your VPS, never shared with a middleman
  • Native iOS and macOS apps for one-tap provisioning and pairing
  • Tailscale-based private networking between your device and your server

Who it's for

Developers, small teams, and privacy-conscious users who want the ergonomics of hosted AI gateways without handing over credentials or conversation history.

Status
